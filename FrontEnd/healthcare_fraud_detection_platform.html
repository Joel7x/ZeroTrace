Winning Differentiators
🔍 Full Explainability
Every flag has a numbered reasoning chain. Investigators don't just see a score — they see the evidence. Regulators love this.
📡 Real-time, not batch
Most fraud detection runs overnight. ClaimGuard flags suspicious claims before payment clears — stopping fraud at the source.
🕸️ Graph-based network analysis
Rule-based systems miss collusion rings. Graph ML finds coordinated fraud that looks clean in isolation — a major market gap.
🔌 Zero-integration design
Reads existing claim formats (FHIR, EDI 837) without schema changes. Drop-in deployment with existing payer systems in days, not months.
